About Us

Established on 25th July 1985, MCBS is a leading manufacturer & service provider into Electronics Manufacturing Services (EMS) and have been working with many renowned customers like ISRO, Velankani, Masibus, Amtech, Vinrox, Techno Electromech, Mantra Softech (India) Pvt. Ltd., Genus Lighting and several other reputed organizations pan India. MCBS has State of the Art, high end, high-tech and high speed SMT & MI facility including Hi-Rel manufacturing tools and systems to undertake any complex electronics projects in various areas of Space (Space grade), Defence (MIL grade), Industrial and Commercial / Consumer Electronics areas.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision: MCBS-EMS would like to emerge as a leading manufacturing and service provider for various areas of Electronics industry including Space and Defence.

Mission: Cost effective quality products & solutions using State of the Art Technology and Innovations would be paramount in our work culture. Customer satisfaction and concerted efforts shall be key to our success, progress and growth. MCBS-EMS will be committed to comply with the requirements and continually improve the effectiveness of quality management system to achieve the goals.
