Infrastructure of MCBS-EMS

MCBS-EMS is located in a pollution-free electronics area of 10,000 square meters (100,000 square feet) with a built-up area of 60,000 square feet, which houses manufacturing, assembly and integration, R&D facilities, marketing, warehouses, and other departments.

  • Clean room environment with 1,00,000 standard
